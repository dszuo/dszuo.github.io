About
I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student at the Microelectronics Thrust of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) , advised by Prof. Yuzhe Ma. I received my M.S. degree from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2022, and B.E. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2021. My research interests are on the cross-disciplinary study of electronic design automation (EDA) and machine learning, including machine learning-aided VLSI design, and datapath circuit design optimization.
